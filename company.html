Company

Sakari Games was founded in 2008 by a small team of experienced and talented game professionals. The development team's track record shows its capability in creating high quality game content.

The company's main vision is to create "out of the box" game titles using a tool chain based on both open-source and licensed software.

Thanks to its central location, Sakari Games offers close communication and support  when needed for Western-European and UK clients.
