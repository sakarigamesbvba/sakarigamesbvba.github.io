Services

The main activity of Sakari Games is creating high quality game content. This encompasses code and artwork for games, light games or game related products. The team of Sakari Games consists of broad set of freelance game developers with each their unique set of skills.

Sakari Games creates game content, and develops as well as consults on game related projects.

Why work with us? Sakari Games works closely with its customers to achieve results that are on schedule and on budget.

As game content creators, Sakari Games uses their expertise to create the artwork and/or code that fits your projects' needs. Sakari Games can create and implement full features, for example character design, moddeling, texturing and animation for your game project.

As developers, the Sakari Games team creates complete games or offers its services in the prototyping, pre-production, production, optimization and QA phase of your games.

As consultants, the Sakari Games team can help your game projects run smoother and keep the production on schedule and on budget. Our expertise will allow us to analyse the asset tool chain and the bottlenecks of the process of developing game content. This way we can resolve issues that arise during the development of your game and prevent unnecessary delays.
